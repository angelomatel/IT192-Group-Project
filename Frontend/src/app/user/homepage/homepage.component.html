Because every friend matters.

Welcome to save-a-friend! Thank you for having the time to visit this non-profit organization that is aimed to house every man's bestfriend here in the Philippines!.

Save-a-who?

pets
Our shelter has amassed a total of 100 animals of different kinds and are in need of loving parents. These animals have experience pain, hardships, and emotional stress. They deserve all the love and care that they need. That is what our shelter is trying to find and provide for them. The rightful place with the rightful owners.

Save-a-where?

home
Our adoption center follows a strict protocol for finding new parents for our animals. The animals inside our shelter has been through a lot and we do not want them to experience it again. With this, we are trying to find the rightful owners of the animals in order for them to be provided with the best care possible so that they can be feel loved once again and live a happy life.

Save-a-how?

help_outline
Be the rightful owner of these precious creatures by submitting to us your adoption form! After you submit your adoption form, we will check whether you are a fit saviour for our beloved friend and as soon as you are qualified, we will set the date when can you pick-up your new pal.
